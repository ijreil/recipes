Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 big potato
 - 1 medium onion
 - 50 g salt
 - 50 g pepper
 - 150 cl sunflower Oil


Directions:
 1. Fry eggs with 150 cl of olive oil till they are toasted underneath. Medium heat.
 
 2. The onion and potato are peeled, washed, and cut into pieces. Then, they are fried with
    slow heat till they are gold. Once they are ready, they are put in a tray with the fried eggs.
 
 3. Put a slice of cheese over each egg, salt and pepper.
